Help Center

Everything you need to know about the SecureVote Blockchain System

The election moves through three distinct stages managed by the Administrator:

  • Setup: The election is being prepared. Admins upload voter lists and candidates. Voting is disabled.
  • Active: The election is live. Registered voters can cast their votes within the specified time window.
  • Closed: Voting has ended. No more votes can be cast, and the results are finalized for public viewing.

We use a two-step verification process. First, your Voter ID must exist in the authorized list provided by the election commission. Second, you must provide your Aadhaar Number and Date of Birth exactly as they appear in official records to register your password.

Every vote is stored as a "Block" in a chain. Each block contains the Hash (digital fingerprint) of the previous block. If anyone tries to change a vote in the database, the "Chain" breaks, and the Validation tool will immediately detect the tampering.
